Summary

"Inception" is a sci-fi thriller directed by Christopher Nolan. It follows Dom Cobb, a thief who specializes in infiltrating the subconscious of his targets. Cobb is offered a chance to have his criminal history erased if he can successfully plant an idea into a target's mind, a process known as "inception."
